Early 2018, and we are in the midst of introducing a set of new restaurant experiences to the Nordic markets. To help our guests getting more creative and productive days. To tune in the requirements of our modern life. But creating a long-time consistent (brand) experience is not an easy task. Abinaya, Iniya Food services Head of commercial development shares her thoughts and hopes from one of the premiere openings of Iniya Food & Co.

Building a brand takes time, money and patience - and many times it fails. So, you can understand we were nervous later this fall when launching a new brand for our restaurants. Have we thought about all the dimensions? Will people understand? Does the message, and the story we are so passionate about, come across? But most of all – will they love our food and come back day after day, after day …
