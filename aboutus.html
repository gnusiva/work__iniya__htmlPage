About Us

Iniya Food & Co is an independent tire dealer with 450 locations. The About Us page tells a story in a “voice” that reflects the company culture and philosophy. Its tagline embodies the company’s history, as does the owner’s profile and the timeline graphic. A link to testimonials provides social proof. It’s all presented in a way that engages the consumer and evokes a casual, friendly atmosphere.
